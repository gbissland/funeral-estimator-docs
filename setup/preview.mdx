---
title: "Previewing Your Estimator"
---

The Preview page lets you see your estimator exactly as families will — without embedding it on your website first.

To open it, go to **Estimator → Preview** in the sidebar.

## What you'll see

The preview loads your live estimator, including your current pricing, categories, branding, and wording. Any changes you make to your settings are reflected immediately — there's no need to refresh or republish.

<Note>
  If you're on a trial account, the trial watermark will appear in the preview. That's intentional — it lets you see exactly what families would see before you upgrade to a paid plan.
</Note>

## Viewport toggle

Use the mobile and desktop toggle at the top of the preview to switch between views. The estimator is designed to work well on both — but it's worth checking the mobile view, as most families will be visiting on their phone.

## Open in new tab

The **Open in new tab** button opens your estimator in a full browser window. This is handy for sharing a preview link with a colleague, or for seeing it without the admin sidebar around it.

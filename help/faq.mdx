title: "Frequently Asked Questions"
description: "Plain answers to the questions we hear most often from funeral directors."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can families see all my pricing?">
    Yes, and that's the whole point. Pricing transparency helps families feel prepared and builds trust in your business before they've even spoken to anyone. Research consistently shows that families who understand costs upfront are more likely to reach out and more likely to proceed with confidence. The estimator never hides prices behind a contact form.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if I change a price?">
    Changes apply to new estimates immediately, as soon as you save an update, any new estimate a family builds will use the updated price. Existing shared estimates keep their original pricing, so families who've already received a link won't see unexpected changes when they revisit it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data private?">
    Completely. Each funeral home's data is entirely separate from every other account. No one else can see your categories, pricing, enquiries, or analytics. Your information is only accessible to people who log in to your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I have different pricing per location?">
    Pricing is shared across all your branches, each branch uses the same service types and options. Where branches differ is in which staff members are shown to families and who receives email notifications for enquiries. If you need significantly different pricing per location, get in touch and we can discuss your options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens when a family submits their details?">
    You'll receive an email notification straight away with the family's name, contact details, and a full breakdown of everything they selected in their estimate. The enquiry also appears in your dashboard under Recent Enquiries, where you can review it any time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I export my data?">
    Yes. Both enquiries and estimations can be exported as CSV files from their respective pages in the dashboard. This makes it straightforward to pull data into a spreadsheet, share it with your team, or keep records for your own purposes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do families have to give their details to see prices?">
    No. The estimate is always fully visible, no registration, no form, no gate. Leaving contact details is entirely optional, and the prompt to do so only appears after a family has already seen their full estimate. Pricing is never withheld.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'indicative estimate' mean?">
    It means the prices shown are a guide based on the options a family has selected, not a binding quote. Actual costs may vary depending on circumstances. Your disclaimer text (which you can customise in **Estimator → Text & Wording**) makes this clear to families. We recommend keeping it straightforward and reassuring rather than legalistic.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

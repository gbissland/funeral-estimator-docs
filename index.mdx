Funeral Estimator Pro lets you publish a personalised price estimator directly on your funeral home website. Families can browse your service options, build their own estimate, and see a realistic cost breakdown, all at their own pace, without needing to call or visit first. You stay in control of every price, every word, and how it all looks.

## How families experience your estimator

When a family visits your estimator, here's what they go through:

1. **Greeting screen**, They're welcomed by your team and asked a few simple questions, including how many people are likely to attend. This personalises the experience from the start.
2. **Choose a service type**, They select the type of service they're planning, such as a cremation or burial. Each service type can have its own set of options.
3. **Browse categories and make selections**, They work through each section of your pricing, professional services, coffins, venues, catering, and so on, choosing the options that fit their needs.
4. **See their estimate**, A running total is visible throughout, and the final estimate screen shows a full breakdown of everything they've selected.
5. **Optionally leave their details**, If they'd like to hear from you, they can submit their name and contact information. This is always optional, families are never required to give personal details to see your pricing.

## What you'll configure

To get your estimator live, you'll work through a few key areas:

- **Business details**, Your funeral home name, contact information, and location
- **Service types and pricing**, The types of services you offer, and the options within each
- **Categories and options**, The individual items families can select, with descriptions and prices
- **Appearance**, Your brand colours, font choice, and icon theme
- **The words families see**, Welcome messages, section headings, and your indicative estimate disclaimer
- **Your team profiles**, Names, photos, and roles shown on the greeting screen

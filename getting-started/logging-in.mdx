## Your dashboard address

Your admin dashboard lives at **[app.funeralestimatorpro.com](https://app.funeralestimatorpro.com)**. Bookmark it — you'll be visiting regularly.

## Getting your account

Accounts aren't created through a sign-up form. We set yours up for you and send your login credentials by email before you go live. If you haven't received your credentials, check your spam folder first, then get in touch at [support@funeralestimatorpro.com](mailto:support@funeralestimatorpro.com).

## Logging in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the dashboard">
    Visit [app.funeralestimatorpro.com](https://app.funeralestimatorpro.com) in your browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address your account was created with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your password">
    Type your password, then click **Log in**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resetting your password

Forgotten your password? No problem.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click 'Forgot password'">
    On the login screen, click the **Forgot password** link below the password field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Type the email address your account was created with and click **Send reset link**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email">
    You'll receive an email with a link to reset your password. If it doesn't arrive within a few minutes, check your spam or junk folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your new password">
    Click the link in the email, enter your new password, and confirm it. You'll be logged in automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you've tried resetting your password and still can't get in, contact us at [support@funeralestimatorpro.com](mailto:support@funeralestimatorpro.com) and we'll sort it out for you.
</Note>
